About Me

Hello, I'm Natnael Eskinder

A Technology lover from Harar, Ethiopia. Growing up amidst Harar's rich culture, my passion for technology was ignited early on, leading me to pursue Computer Science. Let me share some stories from my high school and university journey that highlight my connection to the world of Computer Science.

High School

My interest in Computer Science started in 7th grade when I joined tech clubs at school. Since then, I've been actively participating in these clubs. In 9th grade, my IT teacher taught me C++, and I even showed my work in class and during family events. But C++ was hard for me to understand completely because I was young.

University

In university, I delved deeper into Computer Science, taking charge of my own learning journey. I joined online classes, earning certificates along the way. Over the past two years, I've been self-teaching various software-related skills. On the frontend, I've mastered HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Tailwind, JavaScript, and React. For backend development, I've become proficient in Python, Django, and SQLite. Currently, I'm expanding my knowledge into penetration testing to advance into cybersecurity.. You can explore some of my frontend development projects in my official portfolio.
